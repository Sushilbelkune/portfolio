My projects

Recent ideas

1. Wastege plastic use in road construction

My project explores an innovative approach to tackle plastic waste pollution by repurposing discarded plastic materials in road construction. By incorporating plastic waste, such as bottles and bags, into asphalt mixtures, we aim to create environmentally friendly and cost-effective road infrastructure. Through laboratory testing and field trials, we evaluate the performance and durability of plastic-modified roads, aiming to reduce material costs and environmental impact while improving pavement properties. This project reflects my commitment to sustainability and innovation in addressing pressing environmental challenges through practical solutions.




2. Use of waste plastic bottles in RCC structure

To explore the utilization of waste plastic bottles as a lightweight alternative reinforcement material in concrete structures, with the objectives of reducing dead load, promoting lightweight construction, and mitigating plastic waste accumulation in the environment.
